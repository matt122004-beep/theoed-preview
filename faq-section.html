Common Questions

Everything you need to know about TheoEducation, its content, and how it works

Access & Enrollment

Who can enroll in TheoEducation?

▼

Anyone with a serious interest in biblical theology and pastoral education. Our content is designed for pastors, theologians, seminary students, and laypeople pursuing deeper biblical knowledge. SDA pastors get free access as part of NAD professional development.

Can I try TheoEducation before committing?

▼

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial on both our Recordings and Live + Recordings plans. You get full access to all 150+ hours of content during your trial. No credit card required to start.

What devices can I use to access courses?

▼

TheoEducation works on any device with a browser: laptops, tablets, smartphones, and smart TVs. You can also download lessons for offline viewing, perfect for pastors with inconsistent internet access.

Content & Curriculum

What topics does TheoEducation cover?

▼

We offer 84+ courses across major biblical and theological topics:

  • Hermeneutics & Exegesis
  • Old Testament (Books, History, Prophets)
  • New Testament (Gospels, Paul, John)
  • Daniel & Revelation (eschatology)
  • Adventist history & theology
  • Spiritual formation & pastoral care
  • Controversial passages & cultural topics

How much content is available?

▼

Over 150+ hours of high-quality instruction from expert theologians and pastors. That's roughly 2-3 years of full-time seminary content, accessible at your own pace.

Is the content NAD approved?

▼

Yes. TheoEducation is NAD-approved and offers CEU credits for professional development. Many courses qualify for continuing education hours toward pastoral licensing and credential maintenance.

Pricing & Billing

What's the difference between plans?

▼

Recordings Only ($6/week): Access to 150+ hours, download for offline, 10-year archive. Live + Recordings ($8/week): Everything above plus live classes 2x/week, Q&A sessions, completion certificates, priority support, and community access.

Can I cancel anytime?

▼

Absolutely. Cancel anytime, no questions asked. Your access continues through your paid period, and there are no hidden fees or penalties. You're not locked into a long-term contract.

What if I'm not satisfied?

▼

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If TheoEducation isn't right for you, we'll refund your subscription cost — no questions asked. We want you satisfied or your money back.

Is there a group/church discount?

▼

Yes! We offer team and institutional pricing for churches, schools, and pastoral training programs. Contact us for a custom quote based on group size.

Technical Support

What if I have technical issues?

▼

Our support team responds within 24 hours. Live + Recordings members get priority support. Email us at support@theoeducation.com or use the contact form in your dashboard.

How long do I have access to courses?

▼

Live + Recordings members get lifetime access to the video library. Recordings-only members have 10-year archive access. You can download and keep your own copies.
