CEUs & Adventist Pastors

Yes. The North American Division recognizes TheoEducation courses for CEU credit at a rate of 1 hour = 0.1 CEUs. Pastors who complete courses receive an official certificate they can submit to their Ministerial Director.
Most conferences provide pastors with $200–$500 annually in Continuing Education Funds. Pastors simply sign up, pay for their TheoEducation subscription, and submit the receipt along with their completion certificate to their Ministerial Director for reimbursement. Learn more on our Pastors page.
