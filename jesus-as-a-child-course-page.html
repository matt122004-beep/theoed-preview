Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this course for? ▾
Anyone curious about the stories early Christians told about Jesus's childhood. Whether you're a pastor, theology student, or lifelong learner, this course brings together canonical and non-canonical texts in a way that's accessible and academically rigorous.
Do I need a theology background? ▾
No. Professor Korpman teaches at a level that assumes curiosity, not credentials. If you read the Bible seriously, you can follow this course.
How is this different from a sermon series? ▾
This is academic-level content — primary sources, original languages, historical evidence, and the full range of scholarly debate. It's the kind of content you'd find in a graduate-level religion course.
How many lessons are there? ▾
12 lessons organized into 4 chapters, totaling 8.5 hours of content. You also earn 0.8 CEU credits (Continuing Education Units), accepted by the NAD.
