The Requirement

The North American Division requires pastors to achieve 2 Continuing Education Units (2.0 CEUs) minimally per year to remain properly educated throughout their careers.

The Provision

Most conferences provide their pastors with a Continuing Education Fund of $200-$500 annually explicitly for this purpose. You have the funds waiting to be used.

Global Access

Whether submitting receipts to your Ministerial Director for NAD reimbursement, or utilizing upfront pastoral book funds in Australia or Europe, your education is covered.

How Reimbursement Works

A simple, straightforward process to utilize your conference funds.

1

Sign Up for TheoEducation

Choose the annual subscription or enroll in a specific class. Pay upfront using your preferred payment method on our secure Stripe checkout.

2

Learn & Earn CEUs

Complete your coursework on your own schedule. Once finished, you will automatically receive an official certificate detailing your earned Continuing Education Units (CEUs).

3

Submit for Reimbursement

Simply submit your TheoEducation receipt alongside your completion certificate to your conference Ministerial Director for full, seamless reimbursement.
