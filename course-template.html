About This Class

In this expert-guided class by an Adventist biblical studies professor, re-discover the seventh-day Sabbath and its complicated history in Ancient Israel and challenging future in Christianity.

Discover why scholars suggest that there are three different versions of the Sabbath commandment. Explore the role that the Sabbath held in Jesus' teachings.

Learn the forgotten value that Sunday has held for Christians both in Scripture and in Adventist history.

Browse Lesson Plan

Discover why scholars suggest that there are three different versions of the Sabbath commandment in the historical texts, and how these distinct variations fundamentally shape our modern understanding.
Explore the role that the Sabbath held in Jesus' teachings according to the Gospel of Mark, evaluating the utilitarian perspective He championed against religious legalism.
Learn the forgotten value that Sunday has held for Christians both in Scripture and early Church history, specifically analyzing the gatherings of the Pauline churches.
A deep theological dive into Hebrews 4, interpreting its implications for the ultimate Sabbath rest and its theological fulfillment in the modern Adventist context.
