The 8-Lesson Curriculum

Everything you need to study the Bible like a scholar. Watch in any order after lesson 1.

1

Foundations: What is Biblical Scholarship?

The big picture. What scholars do, how they think, and why their methods matter for your preaching. Set context for everything that follows.

⏱️ 90 minutes

2

Textual Criticism: Evaluating Manuscript Evidence

Why different Bible versions have different verses. How to spot later additions. What scholars can determine from manuscript study.

⏱️ 110 minutes

3

Historical Context: Reading Texts in Their World

Learn the geography, politics, and culture behind the biblical texts. Why context changes meaning. Using historical background to interpret passages.

⏱️ 120 minutes

4

Literary Forms: Recognizing Genre

Poetry reads different than prose. Prophecy isn't always literal. Wisdom literature has its own rules. Learn to identify genre and read each on its terms.

⏱️ 105 minutes

5

Grammatical-Historical Method: The Exegete's Toolkit

Step-by-step framework for interpreting any passage. Grammar, syntax, word studies, and how to handle ambiguity like a scholar does.

⏱️ 115 minutes

6

Authorship & Dating: Solving Mysteries of the Text

When was this written? Who wrote it? Does it matter? How to evaluate evidence for authorship debates. Real examples from Old and New Testaments.

⏱️ 110 minutes

7

Interpretation Case Studies: Method in Action

Watch Matthew apply the entire framework to real passages. See how scholarship works in practice. Questions answered live from students.

⏱️ 95 minutes

8

From Scholarship to Pulpit: Teaching What You Learn

How to use scholarship in your preaching without losing your congregation. Making academic insights accessible. Building your own approach to biblical study.

⏱️ 100 minutes
