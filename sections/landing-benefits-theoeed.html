What You'll Master

Eight lessons covering everything a pastor needs to study the Bible like a scholar.

📚

Historical Context

Understand the worlds that produced the biblical texts. Geography, politics, culture, and history that shaped Scripture.

🔍

Textual Criticism

Learn how scholars evaluate manuscript evidence. Why some verses appear in later manuscripts. How we know what the original text says.

🗣️

Literary Forms

Recognize genre. Poetry reads different from narrative. Prophecy isn't always literal. Learn to read each text on its own terms.

⚙️

Exegetical Method

The step-by-step process scholars use to interpret any passage. You'll apply this framework to every text you encounter.

🎯

Answer Hard Questions

When your congregation asks tough questions, you'll have a scholarly framework to work through them honestly and confidently.

📖

Lifetime Application

These aren't abstract theories. Every lesson includes real examples so you can immediately apply what you learn to your preaching and teaching.
