Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TheoEducation.

Is this for pastors only? Can I join if I'm not ordained? +

No, it's not pastors-only! TheoEducation is designed for anyone serious about biblical scholarship — lay leaders, theology teachers, seminary students, curious believers, and yes, pastors. You don't need to be ordained to benefit from the content. The community is supportive of all levels of theological engagement.

What's the difference between Pay Per Course and Annual Membership? +

Pay Per Course ($6/week): You pick one course and pay for one week of access. You get lifetime access to that specific course. Great if you want to try us out or focus on one topic.

Annual Membership ($416/year): Unlimited access to all 22+ courses, monthly live Q&A, NAD credits, community access. Best value if you plan to take multiple courses. Works out to about $8/week for all content.

Do I get NAD continuing education credits? +

Yes, if you're an SDA pastor or theology professional. TheoEducation is NAD-approved for continuing education credits. You earn 0.1 CEU per hour of content. With 150+ hours available, that's significant professional development credits. Details about how to claim credits are provided with each course.

What if I don't like it? Can I get a refund? +

Absolutely. We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If TheoEducation isn't right for you, we'll refund your full payment. We'd rather you get your money back than feel locked in. That said, most students stick around — not because of our policy, but because they love the content.

How long do I have access to the courses? +

Pay Per Course: Lifetime access to that course, even after your $6 week expires.

Annual Membership: Full access for one year from purchase, then your membership expires. You can renew or let it lapse.

Lifetime Access: Forever. No renewal needed. Ever. You own access to all current and future courses.

Can I download videos to watch offline? +

Yes. Annual and Lifetime membership includes downloadable lesson videos. You can watch offline on your device — perfect for flights, commutes, or times when you don't have reliable internet. Transcripts are also available for download.

What are the live Q&A sessions like? +

Monthly live Zoom sessions where you can ask Matthew your hardest theological questions directly. He answers everything from textual criticism to controversial doctrinal issues. Sessions are recorded if you can't make it live. It's intimate, scholarly, and honest. Typical group is 20-40 engaged pastors/leaders.

Is this scholarly rigor or defensive theology? +

This is scholarship. Real biblical criticism. Historical-critical method applied throughout. We don't shy away from hard textual issues, authorship questions, or the tension between faith and evidence. If you're looking for apologetics that defend every Adventist belief, this isn't it. If you want seminary-level rigor in an accessible format, it absolutely is.

How do I get started? +

Click "Start Your Free Trial" or choose a plan above. Create your account, and you'll get immediate access to all available content. Browse courses, pick one that interests you, and dive in. No complicated setup. No surprises. If you need help, our support team is responsive and helpful.
