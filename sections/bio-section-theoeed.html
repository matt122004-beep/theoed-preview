Meet Your Instructor

Matthew Korpman

Matthew has dedicated his career to rigorous biblical scholarship and making it accessible to working pastors and leaders. He doesn't believe in dumbed-down theology or defensive apologetics — just honest, scholarly engagement with the Bible.

His approach is simple: Ask hard questions, follow the evidence wherever it leads, and help others do the same. That's what you'll find in every TheoEducation course.

Credentials

  • Master of Arts in Religion — Yale Divinity School (Second Temple Judaism focus)
  • 4 Bachelor's Degrees — Theology, Archaeology, Philosophy, and Religious Studies
  • Adjunct Professor — La Sierra University (Bible and Theology)
  • Published Author — "Saying No to God: A Radical Approach to Reading the Bible Faithfully"
  • Public Speaker — Spectrum Magazine, Adventist Voices, Patheos, academic conferences
"The Bible is complex. Pastors deserve scholarship that honors that complexity — not simple answers that avoid hard questions."
