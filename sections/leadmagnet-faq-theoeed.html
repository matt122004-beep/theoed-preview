Questions?

Why is this guide free? +

We believe biblical scholarship should be accessible to working pastors. This guide is our way of giving back to the pastoral community. If you find it helpful, we hope you'll explore our full TheoEducation courses.

Will you spam my email? +

No. We send occasional updates about new TheoEducation courses, but you control your email frequency. Unsubscribe anytime with one click. We respect your inbox.

What format is the guide? +

The guide is a PDF (about 25 pages). You can read it on any device, print it for your desk, or reference it digitally. It includes worksheets you can fill in or customize.

How long does it take to read? +

Most pastors read it in 15-25 minutes. It's designed to be quick and practical, not a deep textbook. You can reference it again and again as you study Scripture.

Can I share this with my church? +

Yes, you can share it with your congregation or study group. We ask that you don't resell or republish it, but using it as a teaching resource is encouraged. More eyes learning to study the Bible deeply — that's our goal.

What if I want to go deeper? +

This guide is just the start. TheoEducation offers 22+ courses with video lessons, live Q&A sessions, and community discussion. Many pastors use this free guide as a preview and then enroll in our full courses for deeper learning.

Do I need seminary credentials to use this? +

No credentials needed. This guide is written for working pastors at all educational levels. It explains scholarly methods in plain language without assuming prior knowledge of Greek, Hebrew, or advanced theology.

How do I get support if I have questions? +

Reply to your welcome email or contact us at support@theoeducation.com. For deeper theological questions, TheoEducation members get access to monthly live Q&A sessions with Matthew directly.
