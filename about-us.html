The TheoEducation Difference

Why Learn With Us?

Taught By Experts

Every class is curated and led by a trained biblical scholar and historian who knows what the facts are and what misinformation has been spread on that issue.

Critical Thinking

Students are expected in every class to utilize critical thinking skills, including doubting and questioning their instructor. The highest goal is to gain independence of thought.

Unique Curriculum

Every class sets itself apart by specializing in a different focus or discussing profound theological topics for which there are not readily available courses elsewhere.

Matthew J. Korpman

Founder of TheoEducation

"I created TheoEducation for people just like you... taking the knowledge that was once locked in a stuffy classroom and moving it into the comfort of your living room."

If you've got a desire to learn, you're ready to start. As a graduate of Yale Divinity School and an adjunct professor of biblical studies and theology at La Sierra University, I've guided thousands of students ranging from curious churchgoers to seminarians.

This platform exists to provide uncompromised, academic-level Adventist Bible study to anyone with a hunger for the truth.
