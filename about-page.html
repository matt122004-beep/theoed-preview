Our Story

TheoEducation was born from a simple observation: pastors are brilliant, but isolated. A pastor in rural Montana faces the same exegetical challenges as one in Seattle, but lacks access to the same resources—peer networks, scholars, theological dialogue.

We created TheoEducation to solve that. By connecting pastors with expert biblical scholars and a learning community, we're democratizing access to seminary-level theological education.

Today, 600+ pastors across North America and internationally use TheoEducation to deepen their teaching, save hours on sermon prep, and answer their congregation's toughest questions with scholarly confidence.
